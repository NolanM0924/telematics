How We Calculate Your Insurance Rate

Understanding Telematics-Based Insurance

Our telematics program uses real-time driving data to provide personalized insurance rates. Safe driving habits can lead to significant savings on your premium. Here's how we analyze your driving data to calculate your rate.

Primary Scoring Factors

  • Speed Management (25%)

    Maintaining appropriate speeds and avoiding excessive acceleration

  • Smooth Driving (25%)

    Avoiding hard braking and aggressive cornering

  • Time of Day (20%)

    When and how often you drive (rush hour vs. off-peak)

  • Distance Driven (15%)

    Total mileage and trip frequency

  • Road Type Usage (15%)

    Mix of highway and city driving

Rate Calculation Frequency

Your driving score is calculated daily, but rate adjustments are made:

  • Monthly review of driving patterns
  • Quarterly rate adjustments based on performance
  • Annual comprehensive review
